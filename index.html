Our Story

Dahlia Language Centre

Dahlia Language Centre (or PUSAT BAHASA DAHLIA in Malay Language) is wholly owned by Dahlia Education Group Sdn Bhd (947182-P) and was established in June 2011 with a commitment to provide quality education.

Dahlia Language Centre is a cutting-edge language centre and recognised by Ministry of Education (MOE), Malaysia to provide language education.

The centre is located in the heart of Kuala Lumpur's Golden Triangle, just a brief walking distance from the Petronas Twin Towers, offers students the stimulating experience of a city campus with complete urban facilities.

Required Documents for Visa Application

  • 1 photocopy of the applicant's entire passport (all pages including blank ones)
  • Passport should be valid at least 2 years
  • 4 passport-sized photos (white background)
  • Certified copy of high school certificate and transcripts (original language + English translation)
  • Post-arrival medical screening form from verified clinic in Malaysia

Terms and Conditions

Important policies and regulations

Payment Terms

  • All fees paid are non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances
  • Payment must be made in full before course commencement
  • Fees are subject to the prevailing exchange rate on the transaction day
  • Any amount communicated in another currency is approximate for illustration purposes only

Exclusions

  • Penalties, lost items (such as books or certificates), resit exams
  • Some excursion and activities fees
  • International examination fees (e.g., IELTS or Cambridge exams) are not included in the fees structure

Currency

  • All fees are in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR) unless otherwise stated

Changes & Amendments

  • The fees schedule is correct at the time of printing
  • DLC reserves the right to amend, change, modify, or alter fees without notice

Visa Policies

  • Visa application fees are included in the administration fees
  • Subject to changes as per immigration or EMGS fees
  • In case of student visa rejection, fees are non-refundable
  • The visa is initially granted for 6 months but is extendable for an additional 6 months

Attendance Policy

  • Students MUST attend at least 80% of classes
  • Visa will be canceled for attendance below 80%
  • 2 weeks consecutive absence without valid reason = visa cancellation

All terms and conditions are subject to change without prior notice.
